# Nurture Skill

Post-proposal email sequence. Generated when a prospect has gone quiet after a proposal. Diagnoses the stall reason (BUYIN, PROOF, INFRASTRUCTURE, TIMING, or operator-named) and produces a 5 to 7 email sequence over 30 to 60 days addressing that specific stall.

No "checking in" emails. No "I hope this finds you well." Each email moves the conversation by addressing a specific element of the diagnosis. Final email always includes a quiet-exit option.

See `foundation.md` section 5.3.
