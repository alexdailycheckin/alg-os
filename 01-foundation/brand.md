# Brand

Template. Filled version lives at `/workspace/foundation/brand.md`.

## Template

```
# Brand - <Operator Name>

## One-liner

<One sentence. Names the unit of value, not the category. Survives the "what does that mean concretely" follow-up.>

## Mission

<2 to 3 sentences. The bit beyond making money. What changes in the world if this company succeeds.>

## Origin (optional)

<Why this company exists. Founder thesis or trigger event. 3 sentences max.>

## Core promise

<The measurable outcome buyers can expect. Quantified where possible.>

## What we are not

<Negative space. The adjacent categories this brand deliberately does not occupy. Helps positioning.>
```

## Quality bar

- One-liner is not a category description. "We help companies grow" fails. "We turn customer-experience leaks into a quarterly index our prospects benchmark against" passes.
- Mission is not generic ("we want to help businesses succeed"). It is specific to this operator's wedge.
- Core promise has a number or could have one. "Faster" alone fails. "75% reduction in manual brief-writing time" passes.
- "What we are not" is an honest exclusion. "We do not compete on price" is fine. "We do everything" is not.
