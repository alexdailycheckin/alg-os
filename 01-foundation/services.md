# Services and Product

Template. Filled version lives at `/workspace/foundation/services.md`.

## Template

```
# Services and Product - <Operator Name>

## Mode

<Product-recursive | Tool-agnostic>

Product-recursive: this operator has a product whose capabilities can power the agents in their own GTM motion.
Tool-agnostic: this operator does not have a recursive product. Agents in their motion run on best-in-class third-party tools or custom builds.

## Smallest unit of sale

- **Name:** <product or service name>
- **Price:** <fixed | range | retainer>
- **Delivery cadence:** <one-off | recurring | ongoing>
- **Outcome:** <the measurable result the buyer gets>

## Mid-tier offering

<Same fields as above. The bread-and-butter engagement.>

## Largest engagement

<Same fields. The strategic, biggest-deal version.>

## Delivery model

<2 to 3 sentences. How the work actually gets done. In-house team, partners, automated, hybrid.>

## What is excluded

<3 to 5 lines. Things buyers might assume are included that are not. Useful for positioning and proposal accuracy.>
```

## Quality bar

- Each tier is named, priced (or has a defensible range), with a delivery cadence and an outcome.
- "Consulting" alone fails. "A 6-week customer-experience audit, fixed price 30k GBP, delivered as a final report plus 3 implementation sessions" passes.
- The mode (product-recursive vs tool-agnostic) is decided unambiguously. This drives the recommendation engine in Build 3.
