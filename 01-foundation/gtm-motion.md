# GTM Motion

Template. Filled version lives at `/workspace/foundation/gtm-motion.md`. This is the proof of intake completion. If the operator cannot articulate this in one paragraph, intake has not finished.

## Template

```
# GTM Motion - <Operator Name>

## The four-step funnel

Per `foundation.md` section 4.3, every ALG-designed motion follows this shape, instantiated for the specific operator.

### Step 1 - The signature artefact

- **What it is:** <name and brief description>
- **What external truth does it reveal:** <the leak, gap, or benchmark>
- **Data set we use to produce it:** <what data, sourced from where>
- **Production cadence:** <quarterly | monthly | one-off>
- **Who is named in it:** <category leaders, target accounts, public companies>

### Step 2 - The natural next question

- **What does the buyer ask after reading the artefact:** <the question>
- **Why is this the natural question:** <2 sentences>

### Step 3 - The low-commitment first step

- **What we offer:** <product feature, audit, free assessment, sample report>
- **Why it is low-commitment:** <free, fast, no procurement, no contract>
- **What it answers about the buyer:** <the personalised version of the artefact's external truth>

### Step 4 - The full deployment

- **What it is:** <the operator's main service or product offering>
- **What gaps it closes:** <surfaced by Step 3>
- **Typical engagement size:** <reference to pricing.md>

## The one-paragraph version

<3 to 5 sentences. The operator articulates their motion in one paragraph. This is what gets repeated to the next investor, partner, board member, hire.>

Example structure: "We publish [signature artefact] every [cadence] that [reveals truth]. Buyers read it and ask [natural question]. We answer with [low-commitment step]. The full engagement [closes gaps]. The compounding moat is [data and brand equity]."
```

## Quality bar

- All 4 steps are populated with concrete content.
- The artefact has a data set the operator can actually produce, not aspirational.
- The next question is what a real buyer would ask, validated against personas.md.
- The first step is genuinely low-commitment, not "book a 60-minute discovery call."
- The full deployment matches services.md.
- The one-paragraph version reads naturally and is short enough to memorise.

If any of these fail the bar, do not write to `/workspace/foundation/gtm-motion.md`. Loop back to whichever section in the questionnaire is weakest.
