# Team

Template. Filled version lives at `/workspace/foundation/team.md`.

## Template

```
# Team - <Operator Name>

## Commercial leadership

- **Owner of commercial functions:** <name, role>
- **Reports to:** <if not the founder>
- **Key responsibilities:** <pipeline, partnerships, pricing>

## Delivery leadership

- **Owner of delivery:** <name, role>
- **Reports to:**
- **Key responsibilities:** <implementation, customer success, ops>

## Artefact production

- **Owner of artefact / data / content production:** <name, role>
- **Reports to:**
- **Key responsibilities:** <data sourcing, writing, design, distribution>

## Decision rights

<2 to 3 sentences. Who signs off on positioning changes, new artefacts, pricing changes, hiring.>

## Headcount and gaps

<List the gaps where the framework's expectations require headcount or tooling that does not exist yet. Honest is more useful than aspirational.>

| Function | Today | Gap |
|---|---|---|
| <e.g. data production> | <who owns> | <what is missing> |
```

## Quality bar

- Real people named with real roles.
- Decision rights are explicit. "We are flat" fails. "Founder signs anything over 50k, head of delivery signs implementation scope" passes.
- The gaps section is honest. If there are no gaps, sparring fires.
