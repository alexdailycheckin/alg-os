# Pricing

Template. Filled version lives at `/workspace/foundation/pricing.md`.

## Template

```
# Pricing - <Operator Name>

## Floor

- **Smallest deal worth running:** <amount, currency>
- **What it includes:** <scope>
- **Why we hold this floor:** <opportunity cost reasoning>

## Ceiling

- **Largest deal closed in last 12 months:** <amount, currency>
- **What it included:** <scope>
- **Why this is the ceiling for now:** <delivery capacity, scope discipline>

## Typical mid-tier

- **Range:** <amount to amount, currency>
- **What is included:** <scope>
- **Cadence:** <one-off | recurring | retainer>

## Pricing model

<One of: fixed-fee | time-and-materials | retainer | outcome-based | hybrid. With reasoning. 2 sentences.>

## Discounting policy

<When discounts apply, when they do not. Useful for skill output that touches commercial conversations.>

## Anchor points (if relevant)

<Comparison anchors used in commercial conversations. "Our smallest engagement equals one quarter of a senior FTE." Useful for proposals.>
```

## Quality bar

- Numbers are real. "Variable" fails. "Floor 15k GBP, ceiling 250k GBP, typical 40 to 80k GBP" passes.
- Pricing model is decided. "It depends" fails.
- Discounting policy is explicit. Sales hygiene tool.
